Skylar

Gold Laner ONIC Esport

Profile

Skylar is a professional esports athlete known as the gold laner for ONIC Esports in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, recognized for his consistent farming efficiency, sharp positioning in team fights, and ability to scale into a late-game damage core, making him a reliable backbone of the team’s offensive playstyle and a key contributor to ONIC’s dominance in high-level competition.
